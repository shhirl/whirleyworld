About me

I'm Shirley He — an AI product and strategy leader living in Zürich. My first career was personal training in a London gym. My second started with a data science master's in Barcelona (econometrics, machine learning, more GARCH models than anyone needs) and five years in the engine room — models, pipelines, cloud migrations. I grew into the part I love most: turning technical possibility into products people actually feel.

These days that means leading AI at SWISS. Outside of it, I help run WiDS Zürich, mentor whenever I can, and collect boarding passes. I work in English, German, and Chinese — and in spreadsheets, fluently.

Off duty you'll find me mid-marathon, mid-handstand, or mid-book — I read everything from econometrics to novels, and I write. More on that soon.
