The story

Reinvention is my home turf.

My first career was teaching people to squat properly in a London gym. My second started with a data science master's in Barcelona and five years in the engine room — machine learning models, data platforms, cloud migrations across an airline group.

Now I lead AI product strategy at SWISS: deciding where AI genuinely moves the needle, and shipping it. The engine-room years are why the strategy holds — I know which promises the technology can keep.

Nobody is coming to save you. Nobody owes you anything. I decided this early — it made everything simpler.
